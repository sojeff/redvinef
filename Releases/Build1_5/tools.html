The SO:KNO Harvester

Installing the "Harvester" tool in your Browser

  1. Display your Bookmarks by clicking View > Show Bookmarks Bar or View > Toolbars
  2. Drag the button image to your Bookmarks bar
  3. When you are browsing the web, push the "Harvester" button to collect Knowledge to your JumpPad
