The SO:KNO Team

Our Mission

SO:KNO was built by a small talented team located in El Dorado Hills, California, who share a common mission. Our Mission is to transform information into knowledge and make it universally accessible.

We are creating a social movement where people will come to share knowledge. Sharing knowledge ignites inspiration, forms relationships and allows people to be far more creative. We believe by harnessing the knowledge of people and their efforts will change the way the world learns and works.

Our Vision is to unlock the intelligence, passion and greatness of people everywhere.

SO:KNO. A World of Knowledge. Infinite Possibilities.
