SO:KNO Guide

Beta Release v0.1

Knowledge Discovery

Discover a global body of knowledge and the brightest minds around any topic.

Collaborative Learning

Engage with like-minded people from around the world in educational, cultural, and scientific content-driven collaborative projects.

Knowledge Cells

Knowledge Cells are Living Communities of Knowledge that contain content, discussions and people around a topic. Deepen your knowledge and understanding by interacting with others who share a problem, an area of interest, or a passion. Create your own or browse Knowledge Cells created by others and discover even more knowledge and meet new people who share your interests.

JumpPads

A JumpPad is where you store and collect Knowledge Cells. Add and share all the things you care about to your own Personal Jumppad.

Threads

Join and participate in community conversations by adding comments, content links or upload additional content to help shape or add to the discussion.

PersonaGraf

Create a personal journey and a dynamic awareness of your natural strengths and talents. Build a global marketable Persona and become an expert around a topic.

Library

Save and share your personal content e-portfolio with others. With SO:KNO's cloud-based platform, your content e-portfolio is always available and accessible anytime, anywhere you go.

Ranking

Earn social recognition and influence among your peers through the SO:KNO community-wide ranking system. SO:KNO rewards the pursuit and efforts of individual participation to build the body of knowledge.

Curation Toolbar

Use the Curation Toolbar to influence the ranking of content and improve the experience for yourself & others. Add ‘Comments’, ‘Flag’, ‘Like' or ‘Share’ your SO:KNO experience with your Facebook and Twitter friends.

The Harvest Button

Add content you care about and share what you find with others. Become a trusted source through which users find content they value. Everything you add becomes part of your Personal JumpPad & your Library and can be shared with any Knowledge Cells you are connected with.

Connections

Discover and follow interesting people, peers and experts that share similar interests. Expand your network and see what your friends are up to and see how influential they are.
